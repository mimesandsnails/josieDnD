THE CHANNEL OF ARRNA

This channel divides the (primarily) elven continent of Vandor√ªn and the (primarily) dwarven continent of Rundar Tor. Trade routes between the two continents are ancient and well established, and this is a relatively safe and heavily trafficked channel.

The exception is the month between mid-summer, and the final month of summer/first month of autumn when all trade in the channel ceases, clearing the way for the great dragon-turtle migration that happens every year as part of their breeding cycle. Some fool-hardy souls may still dare venture into the channel, but with hundreds of highly dangerous dragons passing through for an entire month straight, it's a chance most aren't willing to take.
