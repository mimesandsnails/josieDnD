THE AUSSIR OCEAN

With cold currents able to travel a little more freely from the Skodmeer Ocean into the Aussir than the Durgarn, the Aussir is much colder and subject to higher waves and fiercer winds than than it's neighbor. Even in the hottest tropics of Achuakia, the ocean remains icy cold. Arwom, Achuakia, and western Nothokejtag, which border the Aussir Ocean, all experience fairly frequent earthquakes and tsunamis from the tectonic activity in the area. Even so, it's often safe than the Sea of Souls, and a not-insignificant portion of trade is done between the southern islands of Arwom and Achuakia/Southern Noros to avoid the perils of the Sea of Souls.
