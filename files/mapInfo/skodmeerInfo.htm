THE SKODMEER OCEAN

The Skodmeer ocean stretches from Isfjell's eastern coast, past Iilordur and the northern coast of Vandor√ªn, to the north-western coast of Arwom. Winters on this sea are bitterly cold, with rough storms that dump seemingly unending torrents of rain and snow upon ships buffeted about by high winds.

Summers are milder, hot and humid in the southern portions of the sea, though often still storm-tossed as colder arctic air currents collide with warmer summer air. The ocean stays bitterly cold year round, and a man-overboard is likely to freeze to death before he even drowns. That is, if the sea monsters don't get to him first.
