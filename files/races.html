RACES OF OUDN

In addition to the classic D&D races, there are a few that appear in additional sourcebooks that are both interesting, and commonly played.

However, since I can't keep track of like 50 different races, I have cherry-picked a handful that I particularly like who populate the world of Oudn in addition to the standard 5E Human, Elf, Dwarf, Tiefling, Halfing, Dragonborn, Gnome, and Half-Orc.

This list is not definitive, there are a handful of other races that may appear; but these are the ones your characters are the most likely to encounter in the cities, towns, villages, and settlements they visit.

  • Deep Gnome
  • Dragonborn
  • Drow
  • Dwarf
  • Elf
  • Firbolg
  • Giant
  • Goliath
  • Gnome
  • Halfling
  • Human
  • Lizardfolk
  • Orc
  • Tabaxi
  • Tiefling
